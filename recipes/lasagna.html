Lasagna

Tender layers of savory ground beef and pork with a tomato sauce. Layered with tender lasagna pasta, smothered with mozzarella cheese.

Ingrediants:

For the Lasagna sauce

  1. 1lb fresh ground beef
  2. 1/2lb fresh ground pork
  3. 1 tsp salt
  4. 1 tsp black pepper

For the Pasta sauce

  1. 2 16oz cans pasta sauce
  2. 1 8oz can tomato paste
  3. 1 medium to large onion
  4. 3 large garlic cloves

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, fully cook the ground beef and pork.
  2. Remove and drain the fat.
  3. Add 2 table spoons evo and add the onion, cooking util translucent.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for about a minute over medium heat.
  5. Add the meat mixture to the pan, pasta sauce, and tomatoe paste. Cover and simmer over low heat.
  6. In a large pot add 8 qrts of water and bring to a boil. Add about 1 teaspoon of salt.
  7. Add 1 16oz package of lasagna noodle to the pot and cook according to directions on box.
  8. In a large backing dish add some of the sauce to the bottom of the dish.
  9. Layer pasta and then sauce for 3-4 layers, add some to the top layer.
  10. Add the mozzarella cheese and bake for 1 hr @ 350 degrees.
  11. Let cool for 10 minutes and cut into portions.
  12. For a nice touch add some fresh basil and parmesian cheese.

Bon Appetit!
