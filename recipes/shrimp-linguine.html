Shrimp linguine

Home made shrimp pasta dinner for a quick healthy meal.

Ingrediants:

For the shrimp scampi

  1. 1lb fresh 25 count and lower for the shrimp
  2. 3/4 cup fresh parmesian cheese
  3. 3 large garlic cloves minced
  4. 1-2 tablespoons butter
  5. 2 tablespoons evo extra to drizzle over pasta.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta

  1. 1lb linguine or regular spaghetti

Steps

  1. In a large pot add 8 qrts of water and bring to a boil. Add about 1 teaspoon of salt.
  2. Add 1 16oz package of linguine noodle to the pot and cook according to directions on box.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until the shrimp curls and just turns pink, 1-2 minutes per side.
  4. Add the minced garlic
  5. Add the linguine to the skillet and stir thoroughly.
  6. For a nice touch add some fresh basil and top with parmesian cheese.

Bon Appetit!
