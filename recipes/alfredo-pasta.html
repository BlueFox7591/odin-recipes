Alfredo Pasta

Home made Alfredo sauce in a simple quick pasta dinner.

Ingrediants:

For the Alfredo sauce

  1. 2 cups heavy cream
  2. 2 cups 1/2 and 1/2
  3. 1/2 cup flour
  4. 3/4 cup fresh parmesian cheese
  5. 3 large garlic cloves minced
  6. 4 tablespoons butter
  7. 1 cup cold water
  8. Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta

  1. 1lb linguine or regular spaghetti

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, make a roux.
  2. Add the 4 tablespoons butter and heat slowly.
  3. Stir in the roux a little at a time, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the 4 cups heavy cream and 1/2 and 1/2 a little at a time and bring to a low simmer.
  5. Heat on low heat stirring constantly.
  6. Add the minced garlic and parmesian cheese. Cover and simmer over low heat, keep stirring.
  7. In a large pot add 8 qrts of water and bring to a boil. Add about 1 teaspoon of salt.
  8. Add 1 16oz package of linguine noodle to the pot and cook according to directions on box.
  9. Add the linguine to the skillet and stir thoroughly.
  10. For a nice touch add some fresh basil and top with parmesian cheese.

Bon Appetit!
